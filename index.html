Brand Story

The Restaurant opened in 1947 after we got the independence from the cruel British rule!! The owners, Aryan and Pururaj had spent many years in the restaurant business as franchisees and they were ready to open an original concept. They had slowly developed a plan over many years of visiting restaurants all over India and the world. Ideas they had collected and designs they had studied were worked out and refined until they had a solid concept. The restaurant was restyled on the site of the former training centre and corporate offices of the Taj Hotel(One of the best hotel in Indian heritage). Previous to that it had been the Mockingbird Restaurant.
